# Participating models

The following is a current list of models participating in SOFIA at different stages. The Tier 1 `antwater` experiment has to be completed as a benchmark test for model uncertainty. For Tier 2 and 3 individual experiments conducted are specifically named. Experiments marked &#9989; are completed, &#127939; indicates ongoing simulations. Additional information on ensemble size, availability of biogeochemical variables or passive freshwater (hosing) tracers is provided as available.
